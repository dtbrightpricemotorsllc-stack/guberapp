What Is GUBER?

GUBER is a two-way street. You can work. You can hire. You can help or get help — all in one place.

It’s built for everyday people, hustlers, shop owners, and companies that need extra help without the usual delay.

If You Want to Work

  • Find work anywhere you are — even when you travel
  • Turn your skills or hustle into income the same day
  • Take short jobs, big jobs, or last-minute calls
  • Build a reputation, reviews, and repeat clients
  • Maybe get hired full-time — or stay your own boss

If You Want to Hire

  • Post what you need and get help fast
  • Hire helpers for skilled jobs or simple tasks
  • See ratings and profiles before you choose
  • Only pay when the job is completed
  • Use it at home, at work, or for your business

Verified Inspections & Safe Buying Tools

GUBER is also a trust system. If you’re buying something you can’t see in person — a car at auction, a vehicle out of state, or a marketplace item across town — you can send a helper to inspect it for you.

Helpers can verify vehicles, equipment, and items with photos, video, notes, and checklists, so you can make smarter decisions before you pay or travel.

Coming soon, GUBER will power a verified marketplace where items are listed with real inspection history attached — giving buyers more confidence and giving honest sellers a competitive edge.

One App. Unlimited Types of Work.

Use GUBER for almost anything: mechanical help, diagnostics, moving, yard work, cleaning, pressure washing, small repairs, errands, inspections, towing, and more. If it needs doing, someone nearby can take the job.
