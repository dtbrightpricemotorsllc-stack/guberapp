Welcome to the GUBER Community

GUBER is a platform built on fairness, respect, safety, and equal opportunity. To keep the community safe and professional, all users must follow these guidelines.

1. Respect Others

  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or intimidation
  • No discrimination based on race, sex, gender, identity, religion, disability, or background
  • No hate speech or slurs

2. No Illegal Activity

  • No drug sales or distribution
  • No sex work, escorting, trafficking, or exploitation
  • No weapons trading
  • No “no questions asked” jobs
  • No transporting illegal items

3. Honest & Professional Behavior

  • No scams, fraud, fake accounts, or impersonation
  • No lying about skills or qualifications
  • No submitting false reviews or ratings
  • No taking payments off-platform to avoid fees

4. User-to-User Jobs

  • All jobs are private agreements between users
  • Be clear, honest, and professional in all communication
  • Use in-app messaging whenever possible

5. Protect Your Personal Information

  • Do not share sensitive details with strangers
  • Use caution when meeting in person
  • Report suspicious or unsafe behavior immediately

6. Consequences for Violations

Breaking these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning notices
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account ban
  • Reports to law enforcement (for criminal acts)

Reporting Issues

If you feel unsafe or encounter violations, contact support immediately at: support@guberapp.com
