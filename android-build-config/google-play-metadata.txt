Full description (max 4000 chars):
  GUBER is a trust-enforced local visibility network that connects people
  to paid work in their community. Whether you need someone to help today
  or you're looking for flexible income, GUBER makes it simple.

  WORK:
  • Browse and accept jobs near you across 7 categories
  • Get paid via Stripe directly to your account
  • Build a verifiable work history with your GUBER Resume

  HIRE:
  • Post a job in under 2 minutes
  • See pre-vetted, locally available workers
  • Pay only when you lock in a helper

  FEATURES:
  • Cash Drops — limited-time earning events in your area
  • V&I (Verify & Inspect) — professional property and vehicle checks
  • Marketplace — buy, sell, and trade locally
  • Trust Toolbox — AI-powered identity verification
  • Referral system — earn rewards for growing your community

  BUILT FOR TRUST:
  GUBER enforces trust at every step. Workers build verified histories,
  and posters know who's coming. No guessing. No surprises.
