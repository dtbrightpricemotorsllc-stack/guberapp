Why I Built GUBER

The Imagination That Saved a Child.
The Grind That Built a Man.
The Vision That Created GUBER.

Long before GUBER existed… long before the neon colors, the logo, or the mascot… there was a boy who survived the world using only one thing: his imagination.

Growing up, Mr. T didn’t have comfort, structure, or a soft place to fall. He didn’t have the resources, the support, or the guidance most kids rely on.

But he had a mind that refused to shut down — a mind that created entire worlds when reality felt too heavy, a mind that could picture happiness when life gave him none.

Imagination wasn’t childish. It was survival.

2016 — The Grind Begins

As an adult, Mr. T built his first real business in 2016. No shortcuts. No handouts. Just hustle.

Then life shifted. The woman he loved entered medical school, and he committed to supporting her — not with talk, but with action.

He drove four hours back and forth between cities for over a year, sacrificing his time, money, business, and stability just to keep the relationship together.

He sold off his tools and equipment, watching the business he'd built fade away piece by piece. But he kept going.

Norfolk → Macon → Mobile… each move demanded a new sacrifice. Each one cost him more than the last.

And eventually, the truth surfaced — the truth about loyalty, love, and everything he thought he was building.

GUBER — Born From the Bottom

When everything collapsed — the business, the trust, the relationship — something awakened inside him.

The child who survived using imagination grew into a man who refused to stay down.

Out of heartbreak, loss, and frustration came a decision:

If the world wasn’t going to give him opportunity, he would build it.

Not just for himself — but for everyone who ever struggled like him.

That decision became GUBER.

GUBER wasn’t built from inspiration. It was built from survival. It became a platform for hustlers, helpers, workers, grinders — people who need opportunity now, not “someday.”

Gubee — The Imagination Reborn

Years later, the imagination that saved him as a child returned with a new purpose.

A new character emerged — Gubee — glowing, friendly, futuristic. A mascot built to inspire the next generation.

Adults will see GUBER as opportunity… but kids will grow up seeing GUBER as a world of heroes.

Gubee represents:

  • help
  • hope
  • hustle
  • responsibility
  • imagination
  • a better future

Gubee is the child Mr. T never got to raise — the symbol of what he wanted to give the world.

This Is the True Origin of Mr. T

A man shaped by struggle.

Driven by sacrifice.

Fueled by imagination.

Rebuilt by pain.

GUBER and Gubee aren’t business ideas. They are the result of a lifetime of survival, learning, rebuilding, and vision.

This is the story of how a child’s imagination built a platform that will change the world.
