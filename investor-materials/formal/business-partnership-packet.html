Partnering With GUBER

GUBER is a trust-enforced local visibility network — verified workers, verified hirers, on-platform payments, and documented proof of every job. Businesses partner with GUBER to access verified local talent, refer inspection and transport demand, and reach an engaged local audience without building any of the trust infrastructure themselves.

Four Ways to Partner

1 · Business Scout Plan

$99/mo unlocks 20 talent profiles per month — local businesses source verified, rated workers directly through GUBER instead of cold-posting and hoping.

2 · Dealership & Asset Referral

Auto dealers, property managers, and asset sellers refer buyers into GUBER's Verify & Inspect network for independent, documented pre-purchase proof — reducing disputes and building buyer confidence.

3 · Transport & Load Board

Carriers and freight brokers join GUBER's verified Load Board — identity-checked carriers, escrowed payment, and GPS-tracked proof of delivery on every load.

4 · Co-Marketing & Cash Drops

Local and national brands sponsor Cash Drops and in-app placements to reach GUBER's engaged, growing local user base (see the Sponsorship & Commercial Opportunities packet for pricing).

What Makes GUBER Different

  • Every worker and hirer completes mandatory ID verification before transacting.
  • Every job carries GPS-verified proof, photo documentation, or a formal Verify & Inspect report.
  • Payments are escrowed on-platform through Stripe Connect — funds move only when work is confirmed.
  • Reputation compounds: a worker's completed-job history is a permanent, portable trust record.
  • Partners access this trust layer without building or maintaining any of it themselves.

How Partnership Conversations Work

  1. Intro call. A short conversation to understand your business and which partnership track fits — Scout Plan, referral, transport, or sponsorship.
  2. Letter of Intent (optional). For deeper or exclusive arrangements, we'll put the outline of the relationship in writing — non-binding, and easy to walk away from.
  3. Mutual NDA (optional). If the conversation involves sharing sensitive data or roadmap detail, we'll sign a standard mutual NDA first.
  4. Onboarding. Business account setup, verification ($49 one-time for business accounts), and Scout Plan or sponsorship activation.
  5. Go live. Start sourcing talent, referring verification jobs, or running your first Cash Drop.
