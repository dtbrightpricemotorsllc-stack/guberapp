Trust & Platform Position

Trust is earned. GUBER documents it — GUBER doesn't assume it.

What builds credibility

  • Completed jobs on record
  • Two-sided ratings
  • GPS-verified proof of work
  • ID + selfie verification
  • Consistency over time

What GUBER is (and isn't)

GUBER is not an employer, does not conduct inspections, and does not background-check on behalf of hirers or guarantee outcomes.

GUBER is a visibility and verification infrastructure layer — a record of what people actually do, not what they claim. The platform doesn't create liability. It creates accountability.
