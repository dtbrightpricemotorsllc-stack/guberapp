How a Cash Drop Sponsorship Works

1 · Fund the Drop

A sponsoring brand funds the cash reward for a Cash Drop — a real-money reward hidden or released at a location in a live market.

2 · GUBER Runs It

GUBER places and promotes the drop to its local user base through the app — no media buy required from the sponsor.

3 · Winner Posts Proof

The winner photographs themselves claiming the reward and posts it publicly, tagging GUBER — organic content the sponsor doesn't have to produce.

Per GUBER's internal revenue model, sponsor dollars on Cash Drops run at roughly a 60% margin to the platform, on top of the reward paid to the winner.
