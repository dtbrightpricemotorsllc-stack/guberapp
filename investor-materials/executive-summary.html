The opportunity

GUBER is the Economic Super App powered by a Human API for Truth — building America's nationwide layer for work, opportunity, verification, and real-world trust infrastructure. As AI-generated fraud, marketplace scams, and remote-buying risk accelerate, the demand for human-powered verification is becoming critical infrastructure. GUBER connects people who need help, work, or verification with verified humans who can provide real-world action and proof — backed by enforced identity, on-platform money, and structured proof of work. Live in production today on web and Android; iOS App Store launch in progress.

The product · seven connected layers

  • Work & Hire — guided job builder, structured pricing, GPS proof, auto-pay-increase
  • Verify & Inspect — on-demand human verification of vehicles, properties, listings, salvage
  • GPS proof engine — location-stamped photos, video, dynamic checklists on every job
  • AI or Not — trust subscription with AI-image detection + verified-human badge
  • Cash Drops — brand-sponsored geo-targeted reward drops (a new physical ad network)
  • Business Tools — verified business accounts, talent scouting, bulk job posting
  • Trust & Safety — ID + selfie checks, sex-offender screening, dispute taxonomy, moderation

How GUBER makes money

  • Platform fees on completed jobs · pay-on-lock-in model
  • Verify & Inspect fees · per-verification revenue
  • Urgent / priority job fees · auto-pay-increase
  • Business accounts · verified onboarding + recurring B2B plans
  • Sponsored Cash Drops · brand-funded, gross-margin sponsorship revenue
  • AI or Not / Trust Toolbox · recurring trust subscription
  • Marketing & visibility tools · featured listings, sponsored visibility
  • Future B2B integrations · API access for insurance, lending, marketplaces, OEMs
