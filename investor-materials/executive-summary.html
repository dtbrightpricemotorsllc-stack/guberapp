Brands sponsor geo-targeted Cash Drop rewards. AI or Not · Trust Box subscription

A standalone trust product live at /ai-or-not. Detects AI-generated images and runs unlimited text verification — exposed as a paid badge on a user's GUBER profile so anyone they're transacting with can instantly see they're a verified human. $4.99/mo recurring subscription, billed via Stripe on web and through the in-app ExternalPurchaseSheet on iOS (Apple-compliant U.S. flow). Same engine also powers GUBER's internal moderation pipeline, so every dollar of subscription revenue rides on infrastructure the marketplace already needs. Why it matters: as AI-generated profiles, listings, and reviews flood every other marketplace, "verified human" becomes a feature people pay for — recurring digital revenue with software-margin economics.
