The opportunity

Hundreds of billions of dollars of local micro-services move through Craigslist, Facebook groups, and cash every year — with zero infrastructure for trust, payment, or proof. GUBER is the trust layer: verified identity on both sides, money on-platform via Stripe Connect, and structured proof of work with GPS-locked photos and checklists. Live on web and native iOS.

What's already shipped

  • 50+ user-facing pages on web + Capacitor iOS app
  • 50+ database tables: jobs, payments, money ledger, disputes, businesses, sponsors, observations
  • Two Stripe accounts (subscriptions + Connect) with split webhooks
  • Push notifications: VAPID web-push + APNs direct
  • Admin panel, dispute resolution, soft-delete + retention

Eleven live revenue streams

  • Job platform fee 20% + 3.2% processing
  • Trust Box subscription $4.99/mo
  • Business verification $49 + Scout Plan $99/mo + add-on unlocks
  • Marketplace boosts (7-day featured)

Defensibility

Verified-identity + proof-of-work dataset that compounds per user. None of the incumbents (Craigslist, TaskRabbit, Uber) collect this. Combined B2B side (Scout Plan + Observations) gives a second revenue engine no peer-to-peer competitor has.

Network effect

Every new verified helper makes the marketplace more useful for posters, more attractive to businesses, and more valuable to data buyers. Cash Drops are sponsor-funded acquisition events.
