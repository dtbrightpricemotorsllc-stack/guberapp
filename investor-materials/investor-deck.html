GUBER · HIRER SIDE
Why Verify & Inspect Exists

$25,000 lost on a vehicle I never saw.

DIMETRIS BOWDEN
Founder & CEO · Greensboro, NC

Before GUBER, I was a small independent dealer. I bought a Mustang Shelby GT500 at a dealer auto auction across the country — Florida to California, $25,000 wired sight-unseen.

The condition report was outdated. The vehicle had modifications I didn't know about. Modifications voided arbitration. By the time the motor was smoking on arrival, the loss was already real.

Auctions move fast. Big buyers have people physically on the ground. Small dealers across the country have a few photos and a condition report written months ago. Rules favor the auction. Loopholes protect them from losing money.

People make expensive decisions every day using incomplete information.
Not because they're careless. Because they lack access.

If I could have paid someone already at that auction to send me real-time photos and videos before bidding, I would have gladly spent hundreds for that clarity. That realization became GUBER Verify & Inspect.
