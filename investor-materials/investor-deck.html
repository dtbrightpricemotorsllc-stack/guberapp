The Human API for Truth.

Software can guess. A verified human at the location, with a phone, and proof, knows. GUBER turns that simple idea into a nationwide on-demand verification network.

How it works

  • Request — anyone (consumer, business, marketplace) requests a real-world check or task.
  • Match — the closest verified helper is matched in seconds.
  • Verify — helper completes the task with GPS-locked photo, video, and structured checklist.
  • Settle — Stripe Connect pays the helper instantly, GUBER takes its cut, requester gets evidence on file.

Every verified job adds to the helper's portable resume and to GUBER's proprietary real-world data layer — both compound over time.

Why this is defensible

  • Two-sided verified identity — incumbents only verify one side, or neither.
  • Structured GPS + photo + checklist proof — a dataset no one else collects.
  • Network effect: every new verified helper expands what GUBER can verify, where, and how fast.
  • The data layer becomes an addressable B2B product over time — insurance, lending, marketplaces, and OEMs all need real-world proof.
