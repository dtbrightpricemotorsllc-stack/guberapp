Local help is broken.

For the person who needs help

  • Craigslist & Facebook groups: anonymous strangers, no recourse, payment off-platform.
  • TaskRabbit / Handy: rigid categories, opaque pricing, limited geography.
  • Phone-a-friend: unreliable, awkward, no proof anything actually got done.

For the person who wants to earn

  • Gig platforms take 25–40% and dictate the work.
  • No way to prove a clean track record across platforms.
  • Cash work has no receipts, no protection, no future.

The result: trillions of dollars of micro-services move through informal channels every year with zero infrastructure for trust, payment, or proof.

Guber is the trust layer.

ID

Verified identity, both sides

Mandatory ID + selfie verification for posters and helpers. No anonymous accounts.

$

Money on-platform

Stripe Connect for every dollar. Escrow on lock-in, payout on confirmation. Fully traceable money ledger.

✓

Structured proof of work

GPS-locked photos, video, dynamic checklists. Every job ends with verifiable evidence.

The result: a local marketplace where strangers can transact safely — because identity, money, and proof are all enforced by the platform, not the user.

How we grow.

Demand-side levers

  • Cash Drops drive viral local downloads — every drop is a free acquisition event funded by the brand sponsor.
  • Day-1 OG tier creates evangelists. Lower fees + retention perks in exchange for early adoption.
  • Performance Shares referral system: referrer gets a share of GUBER's platform fee on referred-user jobs for 30 days. Pure cash, no credits.
  • SEO: structured job pages indexed by Google with clean URLs and an /jobs sitemap.

Supply-side levers

  • Verified-helper directory grows with every job — credentials, ratings, proof history compound.
  • B2B Scout Plan drags businesses (and their bulk job volume) onto the platform. Recurring $99/mo.
  • Observation Marketplace turns passive walking-around into income — a unique on-ramp competitors can't match.
  • Native iOS app in App Store + Sign in with Apple + Apple Pay (queued) maximize trust signals on mobile.
