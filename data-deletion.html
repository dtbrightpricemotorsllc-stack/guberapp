How to Request Account & Data Deletion

GUBER allows all users to request the permanent deletion of their account and personal data. Once deleted, your account cannot be restored.

1. In-App Account Deletion (When Available)

When the feature becomes available inside the GUBER app, users will be able to delete their account directly from the Profile or Settings page.

2. Request Deletion by Email

If you want to delete your account now, email us at:

support@guberapp.com

Your message must include:

  • Your account email
  • Your full name
  • A simple statement: “I request deletion of my GUBER account and all associated data.”

3. What Happens When Data Is Deleted

Once your request is verified, GUBER will permanently delete:

  • Your profile
  • All personal information
  • All messages
  • All job history
  • All uploaded photos
  • All stored location or device data

This process is permanent and cannot be reversed.

4. Timeline

Verified deletion requests will be processed within 7 business days.

5. Contact Us

If you need help or have additional questions, contact:

Email: support@guberapp.com
